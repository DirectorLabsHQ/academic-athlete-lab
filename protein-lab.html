The Structural Defense Strategy

01. Halt Sarcopenia

Post-50, muscle loss accelerates to ~1% annually. Protein isn't just for "gains"; it is the essential raw material required to prevent age-related muscle wasting and maintain physical independence.

02. Glycemic Sink

Muscle is your primary site for glucose disposal. By using protein to protect your lean mass, you maintain your "metabolic sink," keeping insulin sensitivity high and systemic inflammation low.

03. Skeletal Integrity

Protein makes up 50% of bone volume. For the masters athlete, hitting high protein targets is as critical as calcium for bone mineral density and fracture prevention during high-impact training.

Source: Clinical models for Anabolic Resistance in Aging Populations (2025).
