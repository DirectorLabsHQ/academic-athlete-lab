Combatting Anabolic Resistance (50+)

01. Halt Sarcopenia

Post-50, muscle loss accelerates. Protein is no longer just for hypertrophy; it is the substrate for survival. Our model uses a 1.8g-2.2g/kg coefficient to offset the decreased sensitivity to leucine in older tissue.

02. The Leucine Trigger

To stimulate Muscle Protein Synthesis (MPS) after 50, you must hit a "threshold" of ~3g of Leucine per meal. This typically requires 35-50g of high-quality protein per bolus, distributed across 4 main meals.

03. Skeletal Integrity

Muscle is the metabolic organ of longevity. By using protein to protect your lean mass, you maintain a "glycemic sink," keeping insulin sensitivity high and systemic inflammation (inflammaging) low.
