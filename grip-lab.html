GRIP

Research Protocol: Neuromuscular Integrity

The Mortality Proxy

Extensive studies (UK Biobank/PURE) demonstrate that grip strength is a more accurate predictor of cardiovascular health and all-cause mortality than systolic blood pressure in the 50+ cohort. It measures the integrity of the Neuromuscular Junction (NMJ), the system that bridges nerve signals to muscle action.
