The Protocol: How to use

1. Select a Compound Lift: This diagnostic is most accurate for Squats, Bench Press, and Deadlifts.

2. Test to Fatigue: Enter the weight and the number of reps you performed where you could not have completed one more with perfect form.

3. Apply the Data: Use the "Efficiency" readouts to program your next session. For example, use the 80% Efficiency number for high-volume muscle building.
