Academic Methodology

The Academic Athlete Lab utilizes peer-reviewed formulas—including the Brzycki and Epley equations for strength estimation—specifically adjusted for the physiological shifts in athletes post-50. Our goal is to move beyond "standard" fitness metrics into data that respects the unique recovery and metabolic demands of the mature competitor.
