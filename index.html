Safety Protocol Advice:

The Submaximal Advantage

For athletes over 50, true 1RM testing is high-risk. Heavy singles put extreme stress on connective tissues that have slower remodeling rates.

Our lab uses the Brzycki Formula to model your strength based on sets of 3–8 reps. This provides the data you need for percentage-based programming without the joint tax.

Longevity Standards

Maintaining a leg press of 1.9x bodyweight and a bench press of 1.1x bodyweight are key benchmarks for functional independence.

Use this tool to track your "Strength Floor"—the minimum level of force required to protect bone density and muscle mass as you age.
