Academic Overview: The Gravity Tax

Climbing Physics

On flat terrain, raw wattage (aero) wins. However, once the gradient exceeds 6%, Watts per Kilogram becomes the primary determinant of speed. For athletes 50+, maintaining a lean body mass (SMI) while preserving power is the "Holy Grail" of performance longevity.

The Masters Curve

Research suggests that while absolute $VO_2$ max and power output decline with age (~1% per year), the **Power-to-Weight Ratio** can be stabilized through specific resistance training and optimized protein intake, allowing masters athletes to outperform younger peers on steep ascents.
