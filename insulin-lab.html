Understanding HOMA-IR

The Homeostatic Model Assessment for Insulin Resistance (HOMA-IR) is the gold standard for tracking metabolic health outside of a clinical clamp. For athletes over 50, a score below 1.5 indicates high metabolic flexibility—meaning your body effectively uses carbohydrates for fuel and protein for repair rather than storing them as visceral fat.

The Masters Athlete Edge

Insulin sensitivity often declines with age, but high-intensity resistance training creates a "glucose sink" in skeletal muscle. By tracking this metric, you can adjust your carbohydrate timing around training windows to ensure maximum anabolic response while maintaining low systemic inflammation.
