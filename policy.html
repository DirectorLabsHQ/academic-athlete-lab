Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Academic Athlete Lab (the "Site") handles information. The Site is a personal, non-commercial informational project providing self-tracking diagnostic tools for adults 50+. No personal data is collected, stored on servers, or shared.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. The Site operates entirely client-side using JavaScript. Any data you input into calculators or the Streak Lab dashboard (e.g., age, weight, test results) is stored only in your browser's localStorage or session memory. This data never leaves your device, is not sent to any server, and is not accessible to us or third parties.

Standard web server logs may capture basic, non-personal details such as IP address, browser type, and access timestamps for operational purposes (e.g., debugging, security). These are not linked to individuals and are handled according to standard hosting provider practices.

No cookies are used for tracking, advertising, or analytics. No third-party scripts (e.g., Google Analytics, social embeds) are present.

How We Use Information

Since no personal information is collected or stored by us, there is no use, processing, or analysis of personal data. Local browser data is used solely to make the tools functional during your visit (e.g., persisting streak counts in the dashboard).

Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, disclose, or transfer any information because none is collected from users. LocalStorage data remains on your device only.

Security

As no personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers, there are no associated security risks from data breaches on our end. Browser-local data is subject to your device's security (e.g., clear browser data to remove it).

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the Site or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance.

For questions about this policy, refer to the Site's About page or any contact method provided (currently none exists).

This Policy is provided for transparency only and does not create any legal obligations beyond what is required by applicable law for a non-data-collecting informational site.
