Academic Overview: Hydration & The Aging Athlete

The Hypohydration Risk in Masters

As we age, total body water decreases and the **thirst mechanism** becomes less sensitive. Research indicates that by the time an athlete over 50 feels "thirsty," they have often reached a 2% body mass deficit—the point where cognitive function and power output degrade rapidly.

Precision Sodium Replacement

Sweat is more than water. For masters endurance athletes, replacing water without electrolytes can lead to **Hyponatremia**. A standard athletic baseline for replacement is ~750mg–1000mg of Sodium per liter of fluid lost.
