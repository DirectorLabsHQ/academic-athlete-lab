The Longevity Ceiling

The Risk Profile: VO2 Max is the single strongest predictor of lifespan. Moving from the "Low" category to "Elite" can result in a significant reduction in all-cause mortality risk.

Functional Headroom: We lose ~10% capacity per decade. Building a high ceiling at 50+ provides the "buffer" needed to maintain high quality of life into your 90s.

Lab Protocol: Training Cadence

01. Intensity

Focus on Zone 2 base building (80%) and one weekly max-aerobic output session (4x4 intervals).

02. Measurement

Use a chest-strap monitor for intervals. Wrist-based sensors are often unreliable at high intensities.

03. Verification

Retest every 12 weeks. VO2 is a trailing indicator of cardiovascular resilience.
